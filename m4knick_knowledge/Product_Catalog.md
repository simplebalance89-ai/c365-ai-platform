# M4 Knick Product Catalog — Complete Reference

## Company Overview
- **Knick Elektronische Messgeräte GmbH & Co. KG** — Founded 1945, Berlin, Germany
- **M4 Knick LLC** — US subsidiary, Milford, OH (since 2013)
- Core competency: Liquid analytical measurement — pH, ORP, conductivity, dissolved oxygen
- ~100 sales partners and subsidiaries worldwide
- Key contacts: Tony Sandfoss (M4 Knick), Michael Beck (Zoho), Grant (rep firm)

## Memosens Technology (Core Value Proposition)
- Digital, inductive (contactless) connection between sensor and transmitter
- Hermetically sealed, galvanically isolated
- Hot-swapping sensors without process interruption
- Pre-calibration in the lab under controlled conditions
- Sensor diagnostics and predictive maintenance data
- Industry-standard protocol (also used by Endress+Hauser and others)

## Four Measurement Parameters
1. **pH** (0-14 range)
2. **ORP / Redox** (-1500 to +1500 mV)
3. **Conductivity** (from nS/cm ultrapure water up to 2000 mS/cm)
4. **Dissolved Oxygen** (0-50 mg/l, down to 1 ug/l resolution)
